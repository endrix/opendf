What is Open Dataflow?

Open Dataflow is a forum for researchers and engineers interested in dataflow programming in general, and the OpenDF design environment and the CAL Actor Language in particular.

The idea is to create a place for developers to share tools and ideas. To be somewhat of the yellow pages for the dataflow community.

While we are intially focusing on the OpenDF framework, we are very much welcoming similar tools and languages. It is not about creating the biggest dataflow project, but growing the dataflow community.

On this site you will find information on tools, research groups, ongoing projects, documentation & academic papers. We also provide links to forums and software respositories.
